Frequently asked questions

Find quick answers about our products, shipping, returns, and more.

General

Are your products suitable for all skin types?

Yes, our products are designed to be effective for all skin types, including sensitive, oily, dry, and combination skin.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Absolutely! All our products are 100% cruelty-free, and we never test on animals.

Do you offer samples or trial sizes?

Yes, we offer sample sizes for select products so you can try them before committing to the full size.

Order & billing

How can I track my order?

After placing your order, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. Use this number to track your shipment on our website or the carrier's site.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and other secure payment options.

Can I modify or cancel my order after it’s placed?

Orders can be modified or canceled within 24 hours of placement. Please contact our customer service team as soon as possible for assistance.

Shipping

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to many countries worldwide. Shipping rates and times will vary based on your location and will be calculated at checkout.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Expedited options are available for faster delivery.
